About Us

We intend to make a platform for the new mothers to help them during post pregnancy phase . The primary goal is to help women deal with post partum depression which even they are unaware and hesitant of. But of course it is not just restricted to depression but also helps in improving mental health of a woman after giving birth. This platform helps them by suggesting songs, sessions, articles, activities to do to help them in improving their mental health. It also allows to connect new mothers to other women who are facing similar issues and other mothers who have been through this.
